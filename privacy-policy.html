Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

PhantomPin ("the App") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data of any kind.

Location Data

The App uses Android's official Mock Location API to simulate a GPS position chosen by the user. The user's real GPS location is never accessed, read, stored, or transmitted by this App.

Map Tiles

The App loads map tiles from OpenFreeMap (openfreemap.org) over HTTPS to display the interactive map. No user-identifying information is sent with these requests.

Analytics and Advertising

The initial release of this App contains no analytics SDKs and no advertising SDKs. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — required to activate Android's mock location service. Your real location is never read or stored.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_LOCATION — required to keep the mock location active while the app runs in the background.
  • INTERNET — required to load map tiles from OpenFreeMap.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — required to show a notification while the foreground service is active (Android 13+).

Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we add features that collect data (such as advertising SDKs), this policy will be updated before those features are released.
